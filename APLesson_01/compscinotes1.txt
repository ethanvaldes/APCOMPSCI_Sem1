escape characters in java
* a character with a backslash (\) before it
* it has a special meaning to the compiler and will not print verbatim
* ex:
* \t (inserts a tab)
* \b (inserts a backspace)
* \n (inserts a newline
* \r (inserts a carriage return)
* \f (inserts a formfeed)
* \’ (inserts a single quotation mark)
* \” (inserts a double quotation mark)
* \\ (inserts a backslash)


ASCII art
* graphic design technique that uses computers to combine different sequences of 95 printable characters in order to form pictures
* basically text based art
